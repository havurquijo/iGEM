Citrus fruits are cultivated in over 140 countries.

They are among the most popular and widely produced fruit crops worldwide!

Citrus and its products are a rich source of vitamins, minerals and dietary fibre that are essential for overall nutritional well-being.

In addition to being nutritionally important worldwide, citrus fruits are essential to the economies of several countries, especially Brazil, the largest exporter of orange juice.

However, in recent years, citrus fruit production has declined significantly due to pre- and post-harvest diseases.

Currently there is no efficient method for controlling or detecting this disease, leading to environmental risks due to excessive pesticide use.

To address this, Pepcitrus proposes sustainable and innovative strategies to combat citrus diseases.

Our solution

Using antimicrobial peptides (AMPs), which are small proteins with antimicrobial activity, produced and delivered through biotechnological platforms as sustainable tools to control citrus diseases and reduce reliance on chemical inputs.
